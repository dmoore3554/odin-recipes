Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These are some tasty chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix sugar and butter in a medium bowl
  2. Add chocolate chips
  3. Scoops dough onto pan
  4. Bake for 10-12min
  5. Let cool for 5min
