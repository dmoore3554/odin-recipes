Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. Add spaghetti noodles and cook till ready
  3. Cook ground beef
  4. Add spaghetti sauce to beef
  5. Stir in chopped onion
  6. Combine and enjoy
